# Tastykake (tastykake)

Tastykake is a Philadelphia-area baked snacks brand founded in 1914, known for Kandy Kakes, Krimpets, Juniors, and snack pies. It has been owned by Flowers Foods (NYSE: FLO) since 2011 and sits in the Flowers Foods brand portfolio alongside Nature's Own, Wonder, and Dave's Killer Bread.

## APIs

No public, developer-facing APIs have been identified for the Tastykake brand. The consumer site (tastykake.com) is limited to product information, recipes, a store locator, merchandise, and a contact form. Partner, retailer, and distribution integrations are handled through parent company Flowers Foods via internal EDI and account-managed channels rather than a public developer portal.
