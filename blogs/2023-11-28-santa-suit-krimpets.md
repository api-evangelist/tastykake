Naughty or nice, you deserve these festive Santa Suit Krimpets! Delicious, legendary Krimpets decked out to look like ol’ Saint Nick. Create this recipe and bring the holiday cheer to your Christmas party or leave some out for the big guy! Prep Time: 30 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Serves: 12 Ingredients 1 box Tastykake Krimpets Red candy melts Black fondant Black round sprinkles Yellow mini square gum pieces Follow these steps: Melt red candy melts according to the package directions. Insert a wooden skewer into the bottom side of the Krimpet so that you can position it over the candy melt bowl. Spoon the melted candy over the entire Krimpet. Shake off the excess. Add black round sprinkles down the front of the Krimpet to make the buttons. Using a pizza cutter, slice a thin strip of black fondant to make the belt. Add a mini yellow square gum piece to the middle of the belt to make the buckle. Enjoy your fun and tasty Santa Suit Krimpets! View More Recipes Permalink
