Feast your eyes on these scrumptious Turkey Krimpets! This recipe is super simple to make and a great treat to enjoy with loved ones on Thanksgiving Day. So, gather up the family and make your tasty turkeys the talk of the table! Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Serves: 12 Ingredients 1 Box of Tastykake Krimpets Candy Corn Candy eyes Yellow candy coated chocolates Red mini candy coated chocolates Orange frosting White frosting Follow these steps: Make your turkey’s tail by placing a dollop of white frosting at the top of your Krimpet, then stick five candy corn pieces to the frosting. Then, place small dabs of white frosting to place the candy eyes in the center of the Krimpet. Apply two final dabs of white frosting to add a yellow candy-coated chocolate candy for the beak and a mini red candy-coated chocolate for the wattle. Using a piping bag, draw two orange turkey feet on the bottom of the Krimpet. Enjoy your finished Turkey Krimpets! View More Recipes Permalink
