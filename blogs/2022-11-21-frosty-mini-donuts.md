Sweeten up your winter with these delicious Frosty Mini Donuts! This simple recipe creates a snowy friend that won’t melt away. Bundle up with the family and build your own tasty snowmen! Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Serves: 6 Ingredients 1 Bag of Tastykake Powdered Sugar Mini Donuts Regular-sized candy coated chocolates Mini candy coated chocolates Wooden skewers Sour strip candy Mini chocolate chips Orange breath mint White candy melts Pretzel sticks Follow these steps: Insert a wooden skewer through three Mini Donuts to assemble your snowman. Create buttons by placing two regular-sized candy-coated chocolates in the center of the middle and bottom donuts. Break a pretzel stick in half and insert each half into both sides of the middle donut to make the arms. Using a pizza cutter, slice a long piece of the sour strip candy for the scarf and a smaller sour strip for the earmuff headband. Heat the white candy melts according to the package directions. Use the melted candy to attach the sour strip headband and two mini candy-coated chocolates to the top donut to make the earmuffs. Make your snowman’s face by placing two mini chocolate chips for the eyes and four mini chocolate chips for the mouth. Place an orange breath mint in the center of the top donut to create the carrot nose. Take a bite and enjoy! View More Recipes Permalink
