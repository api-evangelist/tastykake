Your new favorite fall treat is just around the corn-er. These fun Dipp’n Stalks are made with delicious melted chocolate and crunchy peanut butter candy. Make this super easy treat with the whole family! Prep Time: 45 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Serves: 6 Ingredients 1 box Tastykake Chocolate Dipp’n Sticks 1 bag chocolate candy melts Candy coated peanut butter pieces Non bleached parchment paper Twine Follow these steps: Melt chocolate candy melts according to package directions. Use a spoon to cover the tops of the Dipp'n Sticks with the melted chocolate. Arrange candy coated peanut butter pieces in a fun pattern on top to create corn kernels. Cut parchment paper into six 15”x3” pieces and fold each piece in half lengthwise. Make the corn husks by wrapping one of the parchment pieces around the length of the Dipp'n Stick, and secure the ends together with twine. Repeat for each Dipp’n Stick. Cut the tops of the parchment to fray the ends. Enjoy your chocolate and peanut butter-y Dipp’n Stalks! View More Recipes Permalink
