These irresistible Ornament Swirls are full of Christmas cheer! They’re the perfect holiday treat to make with the family or bring to a festive party. Get creative with your ornaments and don’t forget to leave some out for Santa! Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Serves: 6 Ingredients 1 Package of Tastykake Pecan Swirls Chocolate candy melts Mini peanut butter cups Red pull apart licorice candy White frosting Christmas sprinkles Follow these steps: Pull apart the red licorice candy and cut the licorice strings into 2-inch pieces. Take one 2-inch licorice piece and create a small loop by twisting the ends together. Poke a hole in the top of the mini peanut butter cups with a knife. Push the red licorice loop into the hole in the peanut butter cup. Heat the chocolate candy melts according to package directions. Use the melted candy to attach the mini peanut butter cups to the top of the Pecan Swirl. Now, it’s time to get creative! Use a piping bag to decorate the Pecan Swirl with frosting.
