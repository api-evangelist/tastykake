What’s green, gold, and deliciously bold? It’s this mouthwatering Lucky Kake Shake! A Krimpet and a splash of mint flavor are the secret ingredients to shaking up this St. Patty’s Day. Bring the magic to your family with this fun and simple recipe! Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Serves: 1 Ingredients Rim: 1 Tbsp vanilla frosting Gold Sprinkles Shake: 2 cups vanilla ice cream 1/2 cup whole milk 5 drops green food coloring 1/4 teaspoon mint extract 1 Tastykake Krimpet Garnish: Whipped cream 1 Tastykake Krimpet Shamrock sprinkles Straw Follow these steps: Using a butter knife, spread vanilla frosting along the inside and outside rim of a glass. Pour gold sprinkles onto a small plate. Roll the frosted rim of the glass on the plate to completely cover it with sprinkles. Place the ice cream, Krimpet, milk, food coloring, and mint extract in a blender. Blend until smooth and pour into the glass. Top everything off with whipped cream, a Krimpet, and sprinkles.
