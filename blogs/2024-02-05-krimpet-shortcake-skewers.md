Roses are red, we love Tastykake, these Krimpet Shortcake Skewers are an easy treat to make! You can’t go wrong with delicious Krimpets paired with sweet strawberries and drizzled with vanilla icing. Make this simple, tasty Valentine’s Day recipe for your family! Prep Time: 30 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Serves: 6 skewers Ingredients 1 box Tastykake Krimpets 20 strawberries 2 cups powdered sugar 3-4 teaspoons milk 6 wooden skewers Follow these steps: Cut each Krimpet in half. Wash and dry strawberries, then cut off the stems. Create a skewer by alternating 4 Krimpet pieces and 3 strawberries. In a medium bowl, whisk together powdered sugar and a small amount of milk at a time until the icing is thick enough to drizzle. Place all the skewers on a platter and drizzle with the icing.
